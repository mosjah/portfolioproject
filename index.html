About Me

I am a student at the University of Michigan studying User Experience Design with a minor in Business. I’m passionate about exploring the intersection of technology, creativity, and human needs, which makes product design the perfect outlet for my skills and interests. Along the way, I’ve discovered a love for storytelling, collaborating with others, and creating meaningful, intentional designs. I believe in crafting spaces where functionality, aesthetics, and usability work together seamlessly. My approach is rooted in making everyday experiences more accessible and enriching for all users.
